About me

I’m Rahul Kumar, a Systems Engineer currently working as an L2 Application Support Engineer on the Delta Capita (DC) project. I specialize in L2 Production Support and site reliability for mission‑critical platforms within Managed Services Operations (MSO–NOC), supporting systems that demand high availability, rapid incident response, and strict adherence to SLA/SLO and ITIL practices.

Alongside the Delta Capita engagement, I have hands‑on experience in the DTCC regulatory reporting ecosystem, working extensively with CMRS / ReportHub workflows. My responsibilities include trade workflow monitoring, reconciliation, and exception analysis to ensure data accuracy and audit readiness. During P1–P3 production incidents, I own triage and investigation, perform structured root cause analysis (RCA), and coordinate with engineering, data, and business teams to restore services within defined SLA timelines.

My troubleshooting approach is strongly data‑driven. I use SQL, Azure Data Explorer (ADX), and Azure Cosmos DB to analyze large datasets, detect discrepancies, validate application behavior, and isolate issues across the processing pipeline. From an observability perspective, I leverage Grafana and Application Insights to monitor system health, tune alerts, reduce noise, and improve response efficiency during production events.

Beyond reactive support, I focus on preventive stability and automation. I develop automation scripts using Python and SQL to streamline repetitive operational tasks, reduce manual intervention, and improve consistency in day‑to‑day support activities. I also analyze recurring incident patterns and contribute to corrective and preventive actions aimed at improving long‑term system reliability.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (AI & ML) from M.S. Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences. Alongside my core L2 application support role, I continuously strengthen my expertise across cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, GCP), IT service management tools such as ServiceNow, Jira, and Zoho, and modern data and AI tooling through ongoing learning and certifications.
