About me

I’m Rahul Kumar, a Systems Engineer working in L2 Application Support and production reliability for enterprise‑scale, cloud‑hosted platforms. My work spans complex client environments including DTCC, MSO–NOC, and the Delta Capita (DC) project, where system availability, data accuracy, and operational resilience are critical.

I primarily support applications deployed across multi‑cloud environments (Azure, AWS, and GCP), with a strong focus on stability, monitoring, and incident response. I handle P1–P3 production incidents end‑to‑end, performing investigation, root cause analysis (RCA), and coordinated recovery while ensuring adherence to SLA, SLO, and ITIL‑aligned support processes.

A key part of my role involves supporting DTCC / DC regulatory reporting and trade processing workflows, including platforms such as CMRS and ReportHub. I work on trade lifecycle monitoring, reconciliation, and exception analysis to ensure reporting accuracy, consistency, and audit readiness within highly regulated environments.

My troubleshooting approach is deeply data‑driven. I use SQL, Azure Data Explorer (ADX), and Azure Cosmos DB to analyze large datasets, identify discrepancies, validate processing behavior, and isolate application‑level or data‑level issues. This allows me to resolve problems systematically rather than relying on temporary workarounds.

From an observability standpoint, I work extensively with Grafana and Application Insights to monitor application health, tune alerts, reduce noise, and improve detection time during production issues. I also develop automation scripts using Python and SQL to streamline repetitive operational tasks, improve consistency, and reduce manual intervention in day‑to‑day support activities.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (AI & ML) from M.S. Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences. Alongside my application support responsibilities, I continue to strengthen my expertise in cloud operations, monitoring, automation, and modern data platforms through hands‑on work and continuous professional learning.
